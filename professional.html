Academic activities

Since 2012 I have been a demonstrator for the MECH515/533 - Robotics and Control MSc Robotics module. In this module I supervise students as they learn to automate an Industrial Robotic Arm. From 2012-2013 the students used the Microsoft Kinect (using C#.Net) and Mitsubishi's MELFA Basic to map body positions to robotic arm movements. Recently, students have been using Visual Components 3DAutomate software to create large industrial solutions.
