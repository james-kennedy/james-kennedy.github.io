James Kennedy

Research Scientist

I am a Senior Research Scientist at Apple, working for the Perception and Interaction Engineering group under Machine Intelligence Neural Design (AIML), led by Gierad Laput.

Previously, I was a Senior Research Scientist at Disney Research in Los Angeles. My work applied state-of-the-art machine learning techniques for audio and natural language tasks in the context of interactions with artificial agents. I was also a Post Doctoral Associate at Disney Research in Pittsburgh, USA. I worked on language-based character interaction and AI as part of Jill Fain Lehman's team.

I have also worked on robots for healthcare as a Senior Software Engineer at Futronics (NA) Corp.

I completed my PhD in Human-Robot Interaction at Plymouth University, U.K. supervised by Professor Tony Belpaeme, Dr Paul Baxter and Professor Angelo Cangelosi. My PhD work focused on interactive behavior for social robots. During my PhD, I worked alongside the EU FP7 DREAM project, the EU H2020 L2TOR project, and the EU FP7 ALIZ-E project.

Research
