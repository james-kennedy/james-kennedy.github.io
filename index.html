Welcome

I am a PhD student researching Human-Robot Interaction at Plymouth University, supervised by Professor Tony Belpaeme, Dr Paul Baxter and Professor Angelo Cangelosi.

I currently work alongside the EU FP7 DREAM project and the EU H2020 L2TOR project, and have previously been involved with the EU FP7 ALIZ-E project.

The focus of my research is on the use of robots in the education of children. I am considering the role that a robot should assume and the social behaviours a robot should exhibit when trying to increase the knowledge of a child. All of my work strives towards evaluation in real-world, classroom environments.
